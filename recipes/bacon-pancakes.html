Bacon Pancakes

Description

Like Jake the Dog, we're gonna be makin' bacon pancakes! Two great breakfast foods combined in an - you guessed it - indulgent sweet and savory meal.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 1 cup pancake mix
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable or avocado oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • maple syrup, for serving

Steps

  1. Cook bacon in a large cast iron or nonstick skillet over medium-heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned. About 10 minutes. Drain bacon on paper towels.
  2. Combine baking mix, oil, milk, egg, and cinnamon in a bowl with a whisk until smooth. Mix two crumbled slices of bacon into batter.
  3. Heat non-stick skillet to medium-low heat. Cut bacon slices in half and lay two half slices into the pan. Pour batter over each half slice until covered.
  4. Cook until bubbles appear on the pancake batter. Flip and cook until golden brown. Repeat with the rest of the bacon and pancake batter.
  5. Serve with maple syrup and leftover bacon.
